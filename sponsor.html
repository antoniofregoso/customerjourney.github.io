What is the difference between Patreon and GitHub for sponsorship?

In terms of rewards for your sponsorship, there's no difference. You'll receive the same rewards on both platforms.

The main distinction lies in billing and convenience:

  • Billing (VAT): Patreon charges VAT based on your geographic location.
  • Integration: If you already use GitHub, your sponsorship will be billed alongside your current GitHub subscription, simplifying the process.
In short, the best option for you is the one that's easiest for you. If you're already in the GitHub ecosystem, sponsoring through that platform may be more convenient.
